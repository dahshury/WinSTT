---
title: WinSTT
description: Local-first speech-to-text for macOS, Linux, and Windows — real-time dictation powered by on-device AI, with optional LLM cleanup and text-to-speech. Your audio never leaves your machine.
---

WinSTT turns your voice into text anywhere you can type. Press a hotkey, speak, and the
transcription is pasted straight into the app you were using — a chat box, an editor, a
terminal, a search bar. Speech recognition runs on-device in a single native app;
**nothing is sent to the cloud.**

<Video src="main" chrome="card" alt="The WinSTT main window: the wordmark and active hotkey on top, a live audio visualizer reacting to speech with the transcription appearing below, and a footer showing the GPU/device/model." caption="The main window flow — press the hotkey and speak; the visualizer reacts and the transcription appears live." />

<Callout type="success" title="Your voice stays on your machine">
  Transcription runs entirely on local hardware. Audio is processed in memory by on-device
  ONNX models and never written to disk or uploaded — there are no usage analytics. Optional
  LLM cleanup runs locally via Ollama unless you explicitly opt into OpenRouter. Anonymized
  crash reports (Sentry) are opt-out. The whole codebase is MIT-licensed and auditable.
</Callout>

## What you can do

<BentoGrid cols={3}>
  <BentoCell span={2} icon="mic" title="Dictate anywhere" href="/docs/dictation">
    Push-to-talk, toggle, passive listen, or wake-word. A live preview appears as you
    speak; the polished final text lands at your cursor.
  </BentoCell>
  <BentoCell icon="waveform" title="Real-time preview" href="/docs/settings/quality">
    A fast model streams words live while the main model nails the final accuracy.
  </BentoCell>
  <BentoCell icon="brain" title="LLM cleanup" href="/docs/settings/quality#llm-cleanup">
    Reshape dictation with tone presets and custom modifiers — local Ollama, OpenRouter,
    or Apple Intelligence.
  </BentoCell>
  <BentoCell icon="tts" title="Text-to-speech" href="/docs/text-to-speech">
    Read any selection aloud with Kokoro-82M — 54 voices across 9 languages.
  </BentoCell>
  <BentoCell icon="file" title="File transcription" href="/docs/file-transcription">
    Drop in audio files and export plain text or timestamped SRT subtitles.
  </BentoCell>
  <BentoCell span={2} icon="history" title="Transcription history" href="/docs/transcription-history">
    A local dashboard of everything you've dictated — word stats, an activity heatmap,
    search, and karaoke playback of saved recordings.
  </BentoCell>
  <BentoCell icon="dictionary" title="Dictionary & snippets" href="/docs/dictionary">
    Teach it names and jargon, fix recurring mis-hears, and expand short triggers into
    full text.
  </BentoCell>
  <BentoCell icon="compute" title="70+ models" href="/docs/models">
    Whisper, NeMo Parakeet/Canary, Moonshine, Cohere, GigaAM, Vosk, and more — all ONNX,
    swappable from the UI without a restart.
  </BentoCell>
  <BentoCell icon="privacy" title="Local-first" href="/docs/faq">
    Works fully offline. No accounts, no cloud, no analytics. CPU, DirectML, or OpenVINO.
  </BentoCell>
</BentoGrid>
